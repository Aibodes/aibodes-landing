Agent-free real estate

A transaction OS for selling a home directly.

Aibödes is not another portal built to sell your lead back to an agent. It is an AI-guided system for listing, messaging, offers, counters, disclosures, signing, and closing. The goal is direct home sales with structure, privacy, and a dramatically lower cost.

No 6% agent tax

Sellers should not have to give up tens of thousands of dollars just to move through a transaction. Aibödes is built around keeping more equity with the people buying and selling the home.

Guided, not abandoned

Pricing, negotiation, documents, timelines, and next steps should be clear. The Aibödes app is being built to explain each move while the transaction moves forward.

Trust from day one

Private listing photos, controlled buyer access, secure disclosures, and audit-ready records are part of the product vision, not an afterthought added at the end.
